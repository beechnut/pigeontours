Speaking engagements

We talk about pigeons — a lot.

But our unofficial motto is, “It’s not just about pigeons.”

Co-founders and tour guides Hannah Michelle and Aspen can speak about pigeons from a variety of perspectives, including:

  • current events, including misinformation, empathy, and stereotypes
  • history, including ancient and modern pigeon history
  • ecological literacy and environmental education
  • ecology / urban and natural geography
  • science communication

We’re open to giving keynotes, joining panel discussions, or leading educational workshops.
