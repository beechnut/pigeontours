## Pricing

### Standard pricing

Our standard private tour pricing is $25 per person, with a minimum based on the day of the week.

Weekdays (Monday through Friday): $125 minimum<br /> Weekends (Saturday and Sunday): $300 minimum

### Gift economy pricing

If standard pricing is out of reach, please email us with an offer that works for your budget, and also supports our program.

While considering your offer, please keep in mind that we're a small company, and currently rely on tours as our primary source of income.
